Decision-Making Experiment

Study Information

You are invited to participate in a research study on decision-making in group settings. This experiment will take approximately 20-30 minutes to complete.

What You Will Do

You will participate in a decision-making game with multiple rounds. In each round, you will decide how many tokens to contribute to a group project. Your decisions will affect your earnings.

Risks and Benefits

There are no significant risks associated with this study. You may benefit by learning about decision-making in group settings. You will receive compensation based on your performance in the experiment.

Confidentiality

Your responses will be kept confidential. Your participant ID will be used to track your responses, but no personally identifiable information will be collected.

Voluntary Participation

Your participation is voluntary. You may withdraw from the study at any time without penalty.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this study, please contact the research team.

Welcome to the Decision-Making Game

Instructions

You will participate in a decision-making game with multiple rounds.

  • In each round, you will receive 20 tokens as your endowment.
  • You must decide how many tokens (0-20) to contribute to a group project.
  • Tokens you keep remain yours.
  • Tokens contributed to the group project are multiplied and shared equally among all group members.
  • The multiplier is 1.6 (so 1 token contributed becomes 1.6 tokens shared).
  • Your payoff = (Tokens Kept) + (Total Group Contribution × 1.6) / Group Size

Example: If you contribute 10 tokens and your group of 4 contributes 40 tokens total, you receive: 10 (kept) + (40 × 1.6) / 4 = 10 + 16 = 26 tokens.
